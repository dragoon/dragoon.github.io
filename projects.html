ScienceWISE (http://sciencewise.info)

ScienceWISE allows a community of scientists, working in a specific domain, to generate dynamically as part of their daily work an interactive semantic environment, i.e., a field-specific ontology with direct connections to the text of research papers. The SW system includes a number of crucial elements:

  • an expanding collection of eld-speci c community-ranked encyclopedia articles (mostly about physics at this point of time)
  • an ontological structure (concepts and logical relations between them) encompassing this encyclopedia
  • established connections from ontology entries to a vast collection of research papers (arXiv.org)
  • an operational platform, allowing scientists to annotate and conceptually index (bookmark) research papers, link them against the ontology, and validate and dynamically update the ontology through annotations.
